🎅 Santa's Workshop Features

🤖 AI Santa Chatbot

This heartwarming feature is designed for anyone feeling lonely or sad during the Christmas season. Whether you're away from family due to work, studies, or other circumstances, our AI Santa is here to spread holiday cheer!

How to Use:

  • Talk to Santa: Simply start a conversation with "Hi, Santa!" or any greeting you prefer
  • Share Your Feelings: Santa is here to listen and brighten your day
  • Get Holiday Tips: Receive festive advice and heartwarming Christmas stories

📝 Secret Santa Task Manager

A comprehensive tool to help organize all your Christmas preparations and ensure everything runs smoothly.

Key Features:

  • Add Tasks: Easily input new tasks for your Christmas preparations
  • Track Progress: Mark tasks as complete with a simple click
  • Organize: Filter between all, active, and completed tasks
  • Remove Tasks: Delete any tasks that are no longer needed

🎮 Santa Surprise Game

An exciting two-player game where participants compete to find Santa cards hidden among snowman cards!

Game Rules:

  • Objective: Be the first to find three Santa cards
  • Gameplay: Players take turns flipping cards to reveal either Santa 🎅 or Snowman ⛄
  • Scoring: One point for each Santa card found
  • Victory: First player to score three points wins
  • Replay: Start a new game anytime with the restart button
